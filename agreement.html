Terms & Conditions

  1. The Client agrees to use the hall solely for the event type stated above.
  2. The security deposit ($500) is fully refundable within 7 days after the event, provided no damage occurred to the premises, fixtures, or equipment.
  3. Full payment of the rental fee (less the deposit already paid) is due no later than 14 days before the event date.
  4. Cancellations made 30 or more days before the event date will receive a full refund less the security deposit.
  5. Cancellations made within 30 days of the event will forfeit 50% of the rental fee. The security deposit is forfeited on cancellations within 7 days.
  6. The hall must be fully vacated by the agreed end time. Overtime is billed at $200/hour and deducted from the security deposit first.
  7. The Client is fully responsible for all guests, vendors, and catering staff on the premises throughout the event.
  8. No open flames, fireworks, sparklers, or pyrotechnics are permitted inside the hall at any time.
  9. Outside catering is permitted. Use of the Cathedral's licensed bar requires the bar service add-on selected at booking.
  10. Maximum occupancy must not exceed 500 persons (theatre seating) or 400 persons (banquet seating) at any time.
  11. Amplified music must cease by 1:00 AM. All guests must vacate the premises by 1:30 AM.
  12. The Cathedral reserves the right to cancel a booking due to unforeseeable religious observances or emergencies. In such cases, a full refund including the deposit will be issued within 7 business days.
  13. This agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions. Any amendments must be agreed to in writing.

By signing below, the Client acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to all terms and conditions stated in this agreement.
