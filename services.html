Holy Sacraments

The Holy Sacraments are the sacred means by which God's grace is bestowed upon the faithful. Our cathedral celebrates all seven Holy Mysteries of the Orthodox Church.

💧

Holy Baptism

The Sacrament of entry into the Church. Baptism, Chrismation, and First Communion are administered together for all newly illumined Christians.

By appointment

💍

Holy Matrimony

The Sacrament of Holy Matrimony unites a man and woman in Christ. Please contact the church office at least 3 months in advance to arrange your wedding.

By appointment

Divine Liturgy

The centre of Orthodox worship. Every Divine Liturgy is a participation in the eternal, heavenly worship offered to the Holy Trinity. All Orthodox Christians in good standing are welcome to receive Holy Communion.

Every Sunday 10:30 AM

🕯️

Memorial Services

Panikhida (memorial service) for the departed can be arranged for Saturdays of Souls, anniversaries of repose, and other occasions. Please contact the church office.

By appointment & Saturdays of Souls

🙏

Holy Unction

The Sacrament of Healing — Holy Unction (Euchelaion) is administered to the sick and to all faithful seeking God's healing grace. A general anointing service is held during Holy Week.

Holy Week & by appointment

⛪

Orthodox Funerals

The Church accompanies our faithful to their eternal rest with prayers, the Funeral Service, and burial rites. Please contact Father Ioannis immediately to make arrangements.

Contact office immediately
