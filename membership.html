Parish Membership

Join our 116-year-old community of faith

Become a Member

We welcome all those who wish to join our Macedono-Bulgarian Orthodox community. Whether you are of Bulgarian or Macedonian heritage, or simply drawn to the Orthodox faith and our vibrant community, there is a place for you at Sts. Cyril and Methody Cathedral.

For questions regarding church membership, please contact our church office. Our staff will be happy to guide you through the membership process and answer any questions you may have.

As a member of our parish, you become part of a community with over 116 years of history, faith, and tradition — contributing to the preservation and flourishing of the Macedono-Bulgarian Orthodox heritage in Canada for future generations.

Membership Benefits

Sacramental Life

Full access to all sacraments and liturgical services of the Orthodox Church

🤝

Community

Belonging to a warm, welcoming community of faith and cultural heritage

📚

Education

Sunday School for children, church library access, and community events

🗳️

Governance

Voting rights at parish meetings and executive committee elections

🎵

Choir & Music

Participate in the parish choir and musical traditions of the church

🏛️

Heritage

Part of a 116-year legacy preserving Bulgarian Orthodox culture in Canada

Ready to Join?

Contact our church office to learn more about parish membership and begin the process.
