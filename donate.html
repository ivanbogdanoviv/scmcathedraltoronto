Support Our Cathedral

Your generosity sustains our 116-year mission — preserving Orthodoxy, serving our community, and honouring the legacy of Sts. Cyril and Methody.

Home Make a Donation

Why Your Gift Matters

Since 1910, Sts. Cyril and Methody Cathedral has been a spiritual home for the Macedono-Bulgarian Orthodox community in Toronto. For over 116 years, the generosity of our parishioners has sustained this sacred place of worship.

Your donation supports our clergy, maintains our historic building, funds our Bulgarian Sunday School, supports our choir, and enables us to serve those in need in our community.

Your Gift in Action

$25
Provides candles and liturgical supplies for one Sunday service
$50
Supports one month of Sunday School materials for our children
$100
Contributes to church building maintenance and preservation
$500

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver." — 2 Corinthians 9:7
